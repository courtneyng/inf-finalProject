Security

Duo

Duo security is a two-step authentication process that the school put in place to make accounts safer. You have to download the Duo app on your mobile device and set it up through a link the university sends you.

How to be safe with passwords

Two-Factor Authentication
- Two-Factor Authentication is a way to prevent the risk of possible attacks on your accounts. Some use another biometric (a part of the body) such as fingerprint, facial recognition, or voice recognition. Some may send a message to a trusted device or a combination of letters and numbers to enter.
Password Manager
- A password manager saves all your passwords and allows you to set many different difficult passwords without you having to remember them. It is harder for hackers to guess many different passwords instead of getting into all accounts associated with your email/number with your one password with variations.
Creating a strong password
- It is usually recommended that you have at least 1 special character, 1 captialized letter, 1 number, and be at least 8 characters long.

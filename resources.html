Harvey House

The Harvey House is a gender inclusive residence hall for LGBTQ+ Students and their allies. They work closely with many other departments to create a community for those who identify across all identities. It delivers educational, cultural, and social programming aimed at assisting residents in developing a strong sense of belonging.

Typing Resources

Being able to properly type and or type faster can be an asset to anyone, especially a student. You will be able to get papers done faster or look things up in a shoter amount of time to get work done more efficiently.

Typing.com

Typing.com is used to learn how to properly type on a keyboard. You learn where the fingers rest and which letters or numbers they go to. They have their own typing tests as well as lessons that you can choose to learn from.

Monkey type

Monkey type is a website that tests your typing speed. It is a great way to practice and improve your speed. It is very simple and is very easy to restart and keep going at the tests. They have many options such as removing the feature to see if you made a mistake to improve your typing speed or to get your complete raw speed without corrections.

10 Fast Fingers

10 Fast Fingers is another website that tests the speed of your typing. It is used by many people because it is harsher on it's mistakes and grading. It mostly helps with accuracy of typing rather than just speed.

Keybr

Keybr is a website where you can reinforce accuracy and finger placement as it focuses on specific letters that you may have trouble with or would like to practice. It tracks which letters you get wrong more and will slowly focus the words towards those keys. They do not write out any real words usually and really help concetrate on the keys you need help with.

Resume Resources

This is a good way to get started. There are many resources online that can help you start writing one. This website should be pretty useful when you start out.

There are many things that go into a resume and keywords are some of the biggest things to keep in mind. recruiters might not see you as a great candidate based on the word choices and sentences that you use. This website should provide a list of vocabulary that you can use to substitute for others on your resume.

Linkedin

A LinkedIn account is a great way to start finding jobs and start networking. You can narrow down job search results to include certain areas, or types of jobs, and much more. It serves as an online resume for people to see. Recruiters can also reach out to you based on your skillset. It is a valueable resource to take advantage of.

Handshake

Handshake is very similar to LinkedIn where it serves as an online resume however it is integrated with the university and other schools. Recruiters know that many of the people on Handshake are going to be students and will post jobs for that demographic. Some jobs that you can get on campus may show up on Handshake as well.

Here's how you login

  • Step 1: Select University at Albany Sign On, then enter your Net ID and password.
  • Step 2: Confirm your Handshake account through the email account you provided and follow the instructions to complete your profile. Note: your education, work experience, extracurriculars, projects, and skills can be completed later. Your information can be auto-filled by uploading your resume later on. Go to Documents to upload your various documents (Resume, Cover Letter, Transcript, and Other Documents).
  • Step 3: Once you are logged in select your Profile in the left menu then select the Documents tab. You can select Build Profile from Resume in order to have the data from your resume taken and put into the website. You can view this data to select what you want added to your profile.
